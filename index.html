01
EMS Platform
Commercial-grade Energy Management System for BESS, microgrid, and grid-interconnection. Factory pre-configured. IEEE 1547 compliant. Deployed at PepsiCo/SDG&E.
  • 15 strategy modules including peak shaving, arbitrage, demand response
  • C1–C7 interlock chain — hardware-enforced safety
  • Grid-tie, island, and hybrid operating modes
  • Full SCADA integration and remote monitoring
  • Compatible with leading BESS and PCS platforms
02
BESS Hardware
Battery energy storage system hardware for commercial, industrial, and utility-scale deployments. Certified to UL9540 and IEEE 1547. Full integration readiness.
  • 60 kWh – 3+ MWh system configurations
  • UL9540 · UL9540A · IEEE 1547 certified
  • C&I, utility, and off-grid deployment ready
  • Modular architecture for scalable capacity
  • Full BMS integration with EMS platform
03
Grid Infrastructure
Power infrastructure hardware and system integration support for regulated interconnection environments. EPCs, utilities, and large industrial operators.
  • Electrical distribution and balance-of-system hardware
  • Utility interconnection and protection equipment
  • High and medium voltage systems
  • Engineered submittals and documentation packages
  • Long-term supply programs for EPC partners
