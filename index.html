Products & Capabilities

End-to-end energy infrastructure.

01
EMS Platform
Energy Management System
Commercial-grade EMS for BESS, microgrid, and grid-interconnection. Factory pre-configured. Active deployment at PepsiCo / SDG&E under CA Rule 21 and IEEE 1547-2018.
  • 15 strategy modules — peak shaving, arbitrage, demand response, VPP, grid-forming
  • C1–C7 hardware interlock chain — safety enforced at hardware level
  • Grid-tie, island, and seamless black-start transition modes
  • Modbus TCP · DNP3 · IEC 61850 · Modbus RTU
  • PCS: Power Electronics (Spain) · SMA · Sungrow
02
BESS Hardware
Battery Energy Storage Systems
BESS hardware for C&I, utility, and off-grid deployments. CATL and EVE cells. UL9540 / NFPA 855 certified. Full BMS integration with EMS platform.
  • CATL + EVE lithium cells — tier-1 supply chain
  • UL9540 · UL9540A · NFPA 855 certified
  • 60 kWh – 3+ MWh modular configurations
  • C&I, utility-scale, and off-grid ready
  • Subcontract integration — engineered submittals included
03
Power Transformers
EHV / HV / MV / LV Supply
Long-term supply framework for EHV, HV, MV, and LV power transformers. ANSI/IEEE C57 compliant. Factory acceptance testing and engineered submittals standard.
  • EHV / HV / MV / LV — full voltage range
  • ANSI/IEEE C57 — all units
  • FAT (factory acceptance testing) included
  • Engineered submittal packages — EPC-ready
  • Long-term supply framework agreements

Fiber Optic

Active Programs

Deployed across utility and
industrial infrastructure.

Our programs span the SDG&E service territory (CA Rule 21 / IEEE 1547-2018), ERCOT (Texas), PJM (Eastern interconnection), and the Hydro-Québec grid zone — alongside LA DWP and Fortune 500 C&I operators.

We work directly with utilities, system integrators, and large industrial operators on long-cycle infrastructure programs where reliability, compliance documentation, and long-term operational stability are non-negotiable requirements.

Every deployment includes engineered submittals, hardware-level interlock documentation, utility interconnection compliance packages, and active commissioning support from hardware delivery through final acceptance testing.
